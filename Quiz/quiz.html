Disease Remedies

1. Influenza (Flu)

  • Rest and stay hydrated
  • Use antipyretics (paracetamol) for fever and pain
  • Warm fluids like soup and herbal teas
  • Antiviral medications if prescribed

Disease Remedies

2. Common Cold

  • Increase fluid intake
  • Steam inhalation for nasal congestion
  • Lozenges for sore throat
  • Rest and OTC medications like antihistamines

Disease Remedies

3. Viral Pneumonia

  • Rest and fluids
  • Fever reducers and pain relievers
  • Oxygen therapy if needed
  • Antivirals if advised by doctor

Disease Remedies

4. Acute Bronchitis

  • Cough suppressants if severe
  • Steam inhalation or humidified air
  • Rest and warm fluids
  • Avoid smoking and pollutants

Disease Remedies

5. Sinusitis (Viral)

  • Nasal saline sprays or rinses
  • Steam inhalation
  • Painkillers like ibuprofen
  • Short-term decongestants

Disease Remedies

6. Pharyngitis (Strep Throat)

  • Warm salt water gargles
  • Throat lozenges
  • Painkillers for pain and fever
  • Antibiotics if confirmed bacterial infection

Disease Remedies

7. Gastroenteritis (Viral)

  • Oral rehydration salts (ORS)
  • Avoid solid food until vomiting stops
  • Gradual bland food introduction
  • Probiotics for gut health

Disease Remedies

8. Labyrinthitis

  • Bed rest during dizziness
  • Antiemetics for nausea
  • Vestibular suppressants if prescribed
  • Avoid sudden head movements

Disease Remedies

9. Meningitis (Viral)

  • Rest and hydration
  • Pain relievers for headache and fever
  • Antiemetics for nausea
  • Hospital care if condition worsens

Disease Remedies

10. Early Stage Measles

  • Vitamin A supplements
  • Fluids and soft diet
  • Antipyretics for fever
  • Isolation to prevent spread

Disease Remedies

11. COVID-19 (Mild)

  • Isolation and rest
  • Paracetamol for fever and pain
  • Fluids and proper nutrition
  • Steam inhalation for congestion

Disease Remedies

12. Post-Viral Syndrome

  • Gradual increase in activity
  • Nutritious diet and hydration
  • Painkillers if needed
  • Adequate rest and stress management

Disease Remedies

13. Dehydration

  • Oral rehydration solution (ORS)
  • Electrolyte-rich fluids
  • Avoid caffeine and alcohol
  • IV fluids if severe

Disease Remedies

14. Anemia

  • Iron-rich foods
  • Iron supplements
  • Vitamin C to improve absorption
  • Treat underlying cause

Disease Remedies

15. Migraine

  • Rest in a dark, quiet room
  • Painkillers like ibuprofen
  • Cold compress on head or neck
  • Avoid known triggers

Disease Remedies

16. Food Poisoning

  • Hydration with ORS
  • Avoid solid food till vomiting subsides
  • Antiemetics for nausea
  • Antibiotics if severe bacterial infection

Disease Remedies

17. Panic Attack

  • Deep, slow breathing exercises
  • Reassurance and calm surroundings
  • Avoid caffeine and stimulants
  • Short-acting anxiolytics if prescribed

Disease Remedies

18. Vertigo

  • Rest and avoid sudden movements
  • Antihistamines or vestibular suppressants
  • Epley maneuver if appropriate
  • Hydration

Disease Remedies

19. Upper Respiratory Infection (URI)

  • Warm salt water gargles
  • Steam inhalation
  • OTC cold medications
  • Rest and adequate fluids

Disease Remedies

20. Lower Respiratory Infection (LRI)

  • Rest and hydration
  • Antipyretics for fever
  • Cough medications if needed
  • Antibiotics if bacterial cause suspected

Disease Remedies

21. Allergic Reaction

  • Antihistamines
  • Avoid known allergens
  • Hydration
  • Emergency epinephrine if anaphylaxis

Disease Remedies

22. Ear Infection (Otitis Media)

  • Warm compress to ear
  • Pain relievers
  • Antibiotics if severe
  • Keep ear dry

Disease Remedies

23. Kidney Infection (Pyelonephritis)

  • Plenty of fluids
  • Antibiotics (oral/IV)
  • Pain relievers
  • Rest
